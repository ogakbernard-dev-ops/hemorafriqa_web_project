Who We Are

About Hemor Afriqa Ltd

A decade of passion, experience, and excellence in events and branding — right here in Nairobi, Kenya.

Our Story

Built on Passion & Purpose

Founded over 8 years ago, Hemor Afriqa Ltd started as a small event equipment rental company with a big vision — to be the most trusted name in events and branded merchandise in East Africa.

From our humble beginnings handling small community events, we've grown into a full-service company delivering premium solutions for weddings, corporate conferences, concerts, and everything in between.

Our branding division was born from client demand — businesses and individuals who wanted a one-stop partner for both their events and their branded merchandise. Today, we serve hundreds of clients annually across Kenya and beyond.

Every event we set up, every item we brand, carries our commitment to quality, reliability, and excellence.
